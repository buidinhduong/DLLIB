Meet Little Fish, your very own underwater friend! This adorable fish needs YOU to bathe, dress, feed, cuddle, style, and take care of her! Show your Little Fish lots of love and play tons of exciting and fun-filled mini games! You can even snap pics of your favorite moments with your new fish friend! Caring for your Little Fish is just like owning a real pet! Are you ready for an underwater adventure? 


• ADOPT your Little Fish and watch her grow up!
• DRESS her up with stylish outfits and adorable accessories so she's the most fashionable fish in the sea! 
• FEED your Little Fish with sandwiches, soup, candy, hot cocoa and more! Yummy!
• TUCK your Little Fish into bed and turn off the lights!
• BATHE your Little Fish! Give her a bubble bath and brush her teeth! 
• TREAT her with real doctor tools! Make sure she's healthy and strong! 
• PLAY Bubble Pop, Treasure Hunt and more awesome mini games with your Little Fish!  
• ENJOY tons of Little Fish coloring pages and show off your art!
