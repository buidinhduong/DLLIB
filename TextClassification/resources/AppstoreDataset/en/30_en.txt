Absolutely free app for listening to unlimited FREE music!

Search for your favorite music and listen to/stream them right on your iPhone/iPod and iPad.

The app is the portal to millions of free songs, DJ sets, remixes and live concerts.

Key Features:

√ Fully support for iOS8, iPhone 6 & 6 plus!
√ Single tap music streaming
√ Listen songs before adding
√ Accurate search results for artist, DJ, songs, remix or cover
√ Sort by artist, track name, date, duration
√ iPod-Like music playlists
√ Full featured media player with timer and fine scrubbing
√ Album covers
√ Equalizer with BassBooster
√ Lock screen playback controls
√ Background music playback

And many more…
