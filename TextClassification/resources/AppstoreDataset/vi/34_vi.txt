Having some tasks to do that requires Wi-Fi? 
Need to access Wi-Fi of public places? 
Your cellular network is too slow? 

WiFi Chua helps you find nearby Wi-Fi hotspots and share the ones you know. Join us now.

- Search for Wi-Fi hotspot 
- Share your Wi-Fi 
- Vote and update new passwords

- Offline use, no internet required
